Privacy Policy

Plain and simple: here's what we collect and how we use it.

Last updated: February 2026

Who we are

Universal Window Cleaning is a locally owned residential and commercial window cleaning and power washing business serving the Detroit metropolitan area. When we say "we" or "us," that's who we mean.

What we collect

We only collect what we need to give you a quote and do the work. That usually includes:

  • Your name, phone number, and email address
  • Your property's city or address and details about the job (window count, access notes, and so on)
  • Anything else you choose to tell us in the quote form, by phone, by email, or in person

How we use it

We use your information to prepare your quote, schedule and complete your service, and follow up about your job. We may also use your contact details to reach you about upcoming or seasonal service if you've asked us to.

How your quote request reaches us

When you submit the quote form on this website, your details are delivered to us through Formspree, a third-party form service. Formspree processes the submission only to pass it along to our email inbox. We don't run our own database on this website.

Sharing your information

We do not sell or rent your information. We only share it with the everyday tools we use to run the business (like the form service above and our email provider), and only as needed to serve you or when required by law.

Cookies & tracking

This is a simple static website. We don't set advertising or tracking cookies. The site loads a web font from Google Fonts, which may receive standard technical request data (like your browser type) as part of delivering the font.

Your choices

You can ask us to update or delete the information we have about you, or to stop contacting you, at any time — just reach out using the details below.

Contact us

Questions about your privacy? Call or text (248) 971-4494 or email universalwindowcleaning@outlook.com.
