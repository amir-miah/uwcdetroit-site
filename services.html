Frequently asked questions

How often should I have my windows cleaned?
Most homeowners choose twice a year — spring and fall. Storefronts and offices often prefer monthly or quarterly service to always look their best for customers.
Do I need to be home during the service?
For exterior-only cleaning, no — just let us know how to access the property. For interior work, we'll coordinate a time that's convenient for you.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes. Universal Window Cleaning is fully licensed and insured, so you're covered while we're on your property.
What if I'm not happy with the result?
Every job is backed by our satisfaction guarantee. If anything isn't right, let us know soon after your service and we'll come back and take care of it.
Which areas do you serve?
We serve Livonia, Warren, Sterling Heights, Detroit and the surrounding metro suburbs. Not sure if you're covered? Just ask — we likely reach you.
